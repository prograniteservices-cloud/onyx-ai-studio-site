# Product Design Document

## Product
Onyx AI Studio is a premium implementation studio for AI business operations integration.

## Audience
- Operators at small and mid-sized businesses where calls, leads, appointments, documents, and follow-up matter.
- Service businesses, multi-location operators, professional offices, retailers, and companies with repetitive customer questions or internal knowledge needs.
- Buyers who need a managed business system instead of a generic AI agency, web shop, or standalone assistant.

## Positioning
Onyx AI Studio installs AI into business operations by connecting reception, website assistance, lead capture, scheduling support, company knowledge, data handling, and internal employee assistance into one managed system.

Retell is framed as the voice and conversation layer. Supabase is framed as the business data and knowledge layer. The product is the integration, guardrails, workflows, implementation, and management.

## Primary Conversion Goals
- Request an AI Operations Review.
- Understand the flagship AI Business Operations Integration offer.
- Explore reception, website assistant, internal assistant, guardrail, automation, web, and demand-generation service pages.
- Read portfolio proof and insights as evidence that Onyx can build the modules inside the system.
- Understand premium pricing and separate client-paid platform usage.

## Core Pages
- `/`: flagship AI operations landing page.
- `/services`: AI business system overview.
- `/services/ai-integration`: AI Business Operations Integration.
- `/services/reception-web-assistant`: AI phone receptionist and website assistant.
- `/services/internal-business-assistant`: internal employee assistant.
- `/services/guardrails-knowledge-systems`: guardrails and knowledge systems.
- `/services/automation-workflows`: lead follow-up and workflow automation.
- `/services/web-development`: business website and lead capture layer.
- `/services/seo-content-systems`: demand generation content system.
- `/pricing`: implementation and monthly management ranges.
- `/case-studies`: portfolio proof index.
- `/case-studies/vapeos`: AI operations module for messy business data.
- `/case-studies/starry`: voice-led assistant interface proof.
- `/case-studies/unicorn-island`: interactive workflow/product proof.
- `/case-studies/regional-service-site`: lead capture and demand system proof.
- `/insights`: thought leadership around AI operations.
- `/contact`: AI Operations Review request form.

## CTA Strategy
- Primary CTA: "Request an AI Operations Review".
- Secondary CTA: "View the System".
- Pricing CTA: consultation-based review, not checkout.
- Keep CTAs business-focused, practical, and restrained.

## Content Rules
- Do not delete or hide existing portfolio, insights, services, or project proof.
- Reframe existing work around the AI operations offer.
- Avoid hype-heavy claims and promises of perfect accuracy or staff replacement.
- Make public and internal assistant permissions visibly distinct.
- Keep public copy free of personal details from private parent-context files.
